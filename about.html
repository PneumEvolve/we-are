About the DAO

What Is We Build?

A community-focused DAO supporting projects that use blockchain to enable cooperation and shared ownership.

Why We Exist

We envision post-capitalist collaboration where practical tools empower communities to share, grow, and thrive.

How Governance Works

Two tokens power our DAO: SEED rewards participation and contributions, while ROOT grants voting power for long-term decisions.

Voting and Proposals

Proposals are submitted transparently and discussed openly. Community members decide through ROOT token votes.
