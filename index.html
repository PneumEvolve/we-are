1. Introduction

This ecosystem uses four tokens (Seed, Root, Fruit, Branch) with different staking and lock-up periods. A Discord-based Root DAO, approved by WeBuild DAO, manages governance. A Terra Classic-friendly AMM dApp enables trading. The goal is to balance short-term speculation with long-term sustainability.

2. Token Ecosystem Overview

2.1 Token Roles

  • Seed – Tradable meme token with high APY and short lock-ups.
  • Root – Non-tradable governance token earned through staking.
  • Fruit – Stable growth token with long lock-ups.
  • Branch – Intermediary token created from Seed and Fruit.

2.2 Design Principles

  • Align incentives for both traders and long-term holders.
  • Use Branch to manage volatility between Seed and Fruit.
  • Enhance utility through NFTs and community governance.

3. Tokenomics and Mechanics

3.1 Staking Parameters

Different lock-up periods and APY rates encourage diverse participation.

  • Seed: 1–3 months, 20–50% APY.
  • Fruit: 6–24 months, 5–15% APY.
  • Branch: 3–6 months, 15–25% APY.

3.2 Swap Mechanism

Branch tokens mediate swaps between Seed and Fruit to preserve volatility and stability.

3.3 Volatility and Growth Management

High APY keeps Seed volatile while long lock-ups keep Fruit stable. Root ties value to ecosystem activity.

4. NFT Integrations for Real-World Utility

4.1 Seed NFTs: Trading Benefits

Minted by staking Seed; provide fee discounts and other trading perks.

4.2 Fruit NFTs: Loyalty Rewards

Minted after long-term Fruit staking; grant access to exclusive events and higher rewards.

4.3 Branch NFTs: Cross-Staking Perks

Earned by staking Branch; boost Root rewards or reduce vesting times.

5. Discord-Based DAO Integration

5.1 Main DAO Implementation

Root DAO decisions require approval from WeBuild DAO. Discord bots verify token ownership for voting.

5.2 Sub-DAO Creation

Specialized groups manage initiatives like marketing or development.

5.3 Token/NFT-Gated AI Agents

Premium AI services are accessible to holders of Root or specific NFTs.

6. Roadmap

Five phases from foundation (Q3 2025) through expansion (Q3 2026 onward), including token launches, AMM deployment, NFT integration, and community growth.

7. Implementation Strategy

Deploy CW20/721/3 smart contracts, provide liquidity, and use Root DAO governance for parameter adjustments.

8. User Flow Example

A user acquires Seed, trades for Fruit, mints Branch, earns Root, and accesses AI services via NFTs.

9. Potential Enhancements

Gamification, revenue sharing, cross-chain support, and real-world events.

10. Risks and Mitigations

Measures include fair launch protections, contract audits, and incentivizing liquidity providers.

11. Conclusion

This framework aligns speculation with long-term growth through a multi-token system and community governance.

12. Next Steps

Deploy contracts on testnet, conduct audits, gather community feedback, and prepare for the Seed fair launch.

Appendix: AMM dApp Overview

The AMM enables Seed, Branch, and Fruit trading on Terra Classic using CosmWasm contracts similar to TerraSwap.
